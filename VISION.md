# Relationship Intelligence for Boutique Investment Firms

Help small investment teams understand, prioritize, and act on their relationship network so they can raise capital, source opportunities, and manage stakeholder relationships with more context and less manual tracking.

## Who it serves
Founders at boutique investment firms, Investor relations leads, Investment analysts

## Optimize toward
A boutique investment team has a trusted relationship command center that makes their network searchable, prioritized, and actionable without feeling like a heavy CRM.

## Accept work that
- A founder opens the product to see which investor, operator, advisor, or company relationships need attention now and why they matter.
- An IR lead reviews relationship history, recent touchpoints, and next-best actions before planning outreach for fundraising or stakeholder updates.
- An analyst researches a person or firm, sees relevant relationship context, and prepares a concise briefing for a meeting or follow-up.
- A team member captures a new interaction and the product turns it into structured relationship context, follow-up reminders, and useful notes for the rest of the firm.
- A user searches for a target investor, company, sector, or contact and gets a ranked view of warm paths, relationship strength, relevant history, and suggested next steps.

## Close work that
- Do not make the product feel like a broad CRM or mass-email tool
- Do not overload users with implementation details, internal mechanics, or technical workflow language
- Do not expose raw infrastructure, development process, or maintainer concepts in the customer experience
- Do not require users to understand complex setup before seeing value

## Keep it feeling
- Professional, discreet, and high-trust interface suited to finance users
- Dense enough for analysts, but simple enough for founders and IR leads to use daily
- Clear relationship signals, timelines, rankings, and next actions
- Language should feel strategic and human, not like generic sales automation
